Another chapter: Dev Academy

22 Feb 2015

Ah, best intentions that I had of updating my dusty blog regularly…

But now I would really like to try to! I’ve enrolled in Enspiral Dev Academy (as I thought about in my previous post) and am looking forward to blood, sweat and tears, and hopefully a lot of fun while getting better at this development stuff! I want to document my thoughts and learnings as part of the experience, inspired by those in previous cohorts. It’ll be cool to look back and reflect on (hopefully) how far I’ve come, how far we’ve all come.

Wellington is calling…
